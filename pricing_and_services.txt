Key Considerations When Creating Packages:

Target Audience: Define who the package is for (couples, families, business travelers, etc.).
Value Proposition: What makes the package attractive? What benefits does it offer compared to booking services separately?
Pricing Strategy: Ensure the package price is competitive and provides good value while maintaining profitability.
Seasonality: Consider offering different packages or adjusting pricing based on the time of year.
Partnerships: Collaborate with local businesses to offer unique experiences or discounts as part of your packages.
